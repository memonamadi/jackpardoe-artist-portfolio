I am a sculptor and performance artist and have recently graduated from Central Saint Martins with a degree in Fine Art. I make work in abandoned and derelict spaces but recently I have begun to move into more public spheres. My sculptures and performances raise questions about how humans interact with and move through space. Also raised are themes of desolation, dereliction, human struggle and performative labour. I transport and document the journey of my sculptures to their chosen site. I watch and document the resulting encounter between viewer and sculpture, which is then either destroyed or left on site. The documentation is all that remains.

I currently live and work in London. Please do not hesitate to contact me with any questions at: j_pardoe@hotmail.com.
